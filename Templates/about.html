About GNITS

GNITS is a premier institute established in 1997, dedicated exclusively to the empowerment of women in engineering and technology. With over two decades of excellence, we foster innovation, critical thinking, and leadership in every student.

Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where young women thrive in the world of science and technology.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, research and innovation for women engineers.

Values

Integrity, Inclusiveness, Empowerment, and Excellence guide our every step.
